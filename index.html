Introduction

Born in Coventry, raised in Birmingham and lives in New York. Lee Child is one of the world’s leading thriller writers. When he was made redundant from his job as a TV presentation director, he decided to start writing novel. In 1997 Child published is first novel called. Killing Floor and won the Anthony Award awarded for Best in Mystery Fiction and Barry award awarded for Best First Novel.

Twenty-three years on from Killing Floor, Child as writing more than twenty two Books. His thrillers are a publishing phenomenon, dominating the fiction charts, sparking film adaptations starring Tom Cruise, and enthralling millions of fans (ME being one of them).

Lee Child books features a guy called 'Jack Reacher'. Reacher was an ex-army major Military Police, a peripatetic loner who leaves no traces except in the hearts of those he has touched. He is a dynamite, scourge of the wicked, righter of wrongs, a one-man wrecking crew, hurling bad guys into the darkness with breath-taking efficiency. And Trouble always seems to find him.

I first started reading when I was in a really bad place and I didn’t want anything to do with life. I needed an escape from the situation I was in when I stumble upon Lee Child novel. Reading his novel serves as my escape, while I couldn’t escape physically, I could escape into the world of Jack Reacher.
